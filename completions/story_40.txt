This story is about self-determination. It teaches us to know our own professional goals, and take action when needed to reach them. The protagonist aspired for a career abroad and took a chance to get it, pushing for a role on the international team when they told him he wasn't qualified. His story is a testament to the power of self-motivation and determination in creating success. By asking for—and pushing—what he wanted, he was able to achieve the goal he set out for himself. This shows that if you know what you want and put in the effort, you can make it happen!
