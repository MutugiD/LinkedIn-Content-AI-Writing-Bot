When facing rejection in any consulting career, the Imposter Villain (doubt) has a tendency to come out of hiding. But, by embracing the learning opportunity, seeking support, detaching your self-worth from the outcome, and keeping on the move, you can silence that villain once and for all! Rejection may be tough to swallow, but if approached with resilience it can ultimately lead to greater success in the consulting world. So stay determined and keep rolling with the punches.
